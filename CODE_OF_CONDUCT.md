# Code of Conduct

We expect everyone participating in Blob Yard Core to communicate professionally, assume good
intent, and make room for different backgrounds and levels of experience.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, and publication of another person's
private information are not acceptable. Project maintainers may remove comments, contributions, or
participants that violate these expectations.

Report conduct concerns privately to `security@reliability.works`. Reports will be reviewed
confidentially and handled proportionately.
